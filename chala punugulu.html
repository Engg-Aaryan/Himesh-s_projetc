chalapunugulu

Ingredients:

  • Leftover idli batter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
    •

    Instructions

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the leftover idli batter, chopped onion, green chilies, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, baking soda (if using), and salt. Mix well to combine.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai over medium heat.
  • Once the oil is hot, drop spoonfuls of the batter into the oil. Fry in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  • Fry the Chala Punugulu until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides. Use a slotted spoon to remove them from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  • Serve Chala Punugulu hot with chutney or sambar.
