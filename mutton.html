MUTTON CURRY

Ingredients:

  • ½ kg (1.1 lbs.) mutton (bone-in or boneless lamb/goat, cut to 1½ to 2 inches)
  • ¾ tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt (curd)
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon meat masala (or garam masala)
  • ½ teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder (adjust to heat preference, leave out for low heat)
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • ¾ cup (1 large) tomatoes, pureed or chopped
  • 1 to 2 green chilies, slit or chopped (optional, leave out for low heat)
    •

    Instructions:

  • Marinate the mutton with ginger garlic paste, yogurt, salt, turmeric, meat masala, and Kashmiri red chili powder. Cover and rest until you prepare the rest of the ingredients or leave it in the refrigerator to marinate overnight.
  • When you are ready to cook, leave the cold marinated mutton out on the kitchen counter for 30 minutes to bring it closer to room temperature.
  • Sauté finely chopped onions until golden brown.
  • Add pureed or chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Add the marinated mutton and mix well.
  • Cover and cook on low heat until the mutton is tender.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
