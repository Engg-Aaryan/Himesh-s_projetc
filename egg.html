EGG PUFF

Ingredients:

  • 2 sheets puff pastry
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
    •

    Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F).
  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onion until translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  • Beat eggs in a bowl and add them to the pan.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, and chopped coriander leaves. Mix well and cook until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.
  • Roll out the puff pastry sheets and cut each sheet into squares.
  • Place a spoonful of the egg mixture onto each pastry square.
  • Seal the edges of the pastry squares by folding them over the filling and pressing with a fork.
  • Place the filled puff pastry squares on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown and puffed up.
  • Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving.
  • Enjoy your delicious egg puffs!
