PONGAl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup split yellow mung dal (moong dal)
  • 4 cups water, Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • ½ teaspoon black peppercorns
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Curry leaves
  • Ginger (finely chopped)
    •

    Instructions:

  • 1.Wash and Soak:
  • Wash the rice and moong dal together.
  • Soak them in water for about 30 minutes.
  • 2.Pressure Cook
  • Drain the soaked rice and dal. In a pressure cooker, heat 1 teaspoon of ghee.
  • Add cumin seeds, black peppercorns, and a pinch of asafoetida.
  • Add the drained rice and dal mixture.
  • Add 4 cups of water and salt. Pressure cook for 4-5 whistles or until well-cooked and mushy.
  • 3.Tempering:
  • In a separate pan, heat some ghee, Add chopped ginger and curry leaves.
  • Pour this tempering over the cooked Pongal.
