Gutti Vankaya Kura

Ingredients:

  • Small brinjals (eggplants) - 10 to 12
  • Peanuts - 2 tablespoons
  • Dry red chilies - 4 to 5
  • Coriander seeds - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Sesame seeds (optional) - 1 tablespoon
  • Grated coconut - 2 tablespoons
  • Tamarind paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - ½ teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds - ½ teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - a few
    •

    Instructions:

  • Dry roast peanuts, red chilies, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, and sesame seeds (if using) until fragrant.
  • Grind the roasted spices along with grated coconut, tamarind paste, and salt to make a coarse paste.
  • Slit the brinjals lengthwise, keeping the stalks intact.
  • Stuff the ground spice mixture into the brinjals.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Add curry leaves and the stuffed brinjals.
  • Cover and cook on low heat until the brinjals are tender and the flavors meld.
