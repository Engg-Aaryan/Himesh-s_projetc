PARATHA

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons oil (optional)
  • Water for kneading
  • Ghee or oil for cooking
    •

    Instructions:

  • 1.Make the Dough:
  • In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups (250 grams) of whole wheat flour, ¼ teaspoon of salt (optional), and 2 teaspoons of oil (optional).
  • Gradually add water and knead into a smooth, soft dough. Adjust water as needed.
  • Cover the dough and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  • 2.Shape and Cook
  • Divide the dough into small balls, Roll each ball into a thin circle (paratha) using a rolling pin.
  • Heat a frying pan or tawa over medium heat, Place the rolled paratha on the hot pan.
  • Cook for about 1-2 minutes on the first side until you see small bumps.
  • Flip the paratha using a spatula.
  • Brush ghee or oil on both sides and cook for another 1 minute until golden brown.
