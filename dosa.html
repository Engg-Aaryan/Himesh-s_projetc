    • DOSA

    Ingredients:

  • Urad dal (black gram): 1 cup
  • Idli rice (parboiled rice) or regular white rice: 2 cups
    •

    Soaking and Grinding:

  • Soak the urad dal and rice separately in water for about 6-8 hours (or overnight).
  • Drain and grind the soaked urad dal and rice separately to a smooth batter.
  • Mix the batters together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight in a warm place.
    •

    Making Dosa:

  • Heat a non-stick griddle or a cast iron tawa.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot tawa.
  • Spread it thinly in a circular motion to form a dosa.
  • Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  • Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy.
  • Fold the dosa and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
