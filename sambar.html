SAMBAR

Ingredients:

  • Lentils (toor dal or split pigeon peas)
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, eggplant, drumsticks, etc.)
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Sambar powder (a blend of spices)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Mustard seeds
  • Asafoetida (hing)
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
    •

    Instructions:

  • Cook the lentils until soft and mushy.
  • Saut√© mustard seeds, curry leaves, and asafoetida in oil.
  • Add chopped onions, green chilies, and tomatoes.
  • Add the mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  • Prepare tamarind pulp and mix it with water.
  • Add the tamarind water, sambar powder, turmeric, and salt to the vegetables.
  • Simmer until flavors meld together.
