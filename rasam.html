RASAM

Ingredients:

  • Tamarind (small lemon-sized ball)
  • 2 ripe tomatoes (medium-sized)
  • 1 dried red chili
  • 1½ tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • ¼ to ½ tsp black peppercorn
  • ¼ tsp methi seeds (fenugreek)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions:

  • Dry roast methi seeds and one dried red chili until aromatic.
  • Add cumin seeds and black peppercorns, and roast until fragrant.
  • Grind these spices into a coarse powder.
  • Grind these spices into a coarse powder.
  • Add tamarind water, tomato puree, ground
  • Simmer for about 10 minutes until the raw smell of tamarind disappears.
  • Add water and let it froth up.
