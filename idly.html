Idli

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (hulled black gram)
  • 2 cups idli rava (rice semolina) or 2 cups regular rice
  • Salt to taste
    •

    Instructions:

  • 1.Soak the urad dal in water for around 4-5 hours.
  • 2.Grind the soaked dal to a very smooth paste (add minimal water).
  • 3.Wash the idli rava with water (if using homemade rava, skip this step).
  • 4.Grind the rava for a few seconds to make it slightly finer.
  • 5.Mix the ground dal and rava together, add salt, and let the batter ferment overnight in a warm place.
  • 6.Grease the idli molds with oil, pour the batter into the molds, and steam for 10-12 minutes until the idlis are cooked and fluffy.
