BISI BELE BATH

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup rice (short grain rice works well)
  • ½ cup toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  • 1 small lemon-sized ball of tamarind
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
    •

    Instructions:

  • 1.Cooking Rice:
  • Rinse ¾ cup rice and place it in a bow
  • Add 1 & ¾ cups water to the rice bowl.
  • If using a pressure cooker, cover the rice bowl with a plate and cook the rice.
  • If not using a pressure cooker, cook the rice and toor dal together or separately until the dal turns completely mushy.
  • 2.Preparing Tamarind Extract:
  • Soak the tamarind in warm water for about 20 minutes then Extract the tamarind juice and discard the pulp.
  • 3.Cooking the Lentils and Vegetables:
  • In a pressure cooker, cook the toor dal along with the chopped vegetables, tamarind extract, and turmeric powder.
    • Once cooked, mash the dal and vegetables together
  • 4.Making Bisi Bele Bath
  • Heat oil in a large pan, Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add curry leaves, chopped onions, and sauté until the onions turn translucent.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  • Add the cooked dal-vegetable mixture, salt, and the ground bisi bele bath powder.
  • Mix well and simmer for a few minutes. Adjust the consistency by adding water if needed.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, dried red chilies, and curry leaves.
  • Once they splutter, pour this tempering over the bisi bele bath.
