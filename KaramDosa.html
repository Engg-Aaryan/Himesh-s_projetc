KARAM DOSA

Ingredients:

  • Urad dal (black gram): 1 cup
  • Idli rice (parboiled rice) or regular white rice: 2 cups
  • Salt to taste
    •

    Make the Karam (Red Chutney):

  • Grind chopped onions, red chili powder, and salt to a paste (it should not be too fine).
  • Heat 2 teaspoons of oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and stir for a few seconds.
  • Add the onion-chili paste and fry until the raw smell disappears. You can use ghee for extra flavor.
    •

    Prepare Pappula Podi

  • Grind fried gram dal with garlic and salt to a fine powder.
    •

    Cook the Karam Dosa

  • Heat a non-stick griddle or a cast iron tawa.
  • Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot tawa.
  • Spread it thinly in a circular motion to form a dosa.
  • Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  • Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy.
  • Place 2 teaspoons of Karam (red chutney) on the dosa.
  • Add a teaspoon of oil and spread the Karam evenly.
  • Roast the dosa until golden color on medium flame.
  • Fold and serve hot with coconut chutney or buni hui chana dal ki chutney and sambar.
