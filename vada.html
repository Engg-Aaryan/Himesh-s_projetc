VADA

Ingredients:

  • Soak 1 cup of urad dal in water for about 4-5 hours.
  • Drain the soaked dal and grind it to a smooth batter. Add minimal water while grinding.
  • Mix in salt and any optional ingredients you like.

    • Soak and Grind

  • Soak 1 cup of urad dal in water for about 4-5 hours.
  • Drain the soaked dal and grind it to a smooth batter. Add minimal water while grinding.
  • Mix in salt and any optional ingredients you like.

    • Shape and Fry

  • Heat oil in a deep pan or kadhai. Wet your hands and take a small portion of the batter.
  • Shape it into a donut shape with a hole in the center.
  • Gently slide the shaped vada into the hot oil.
  • Fry on medium to low heat until golden brown on both sides.
  • Remove with a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
