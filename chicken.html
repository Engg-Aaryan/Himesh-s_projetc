CHICKEN CURRY

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (drumsticks and thighs)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt
    •

    Instructions:

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a heavy-bottomed pan.
  • Add 1 small bay leaf, 2-inch cinnamon piece, 4 cloves, and 3 green cardamoms (optional but recommended).
  • Saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Add spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder) and salt. Mix well.
  • Add chicken pieces and coat them with the spice mixture.
  • Cover and cook on low heat until the chicken is tender.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
