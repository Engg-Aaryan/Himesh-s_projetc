RASMALAI

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped pistachios
  • 1 tablespoon chopped almonds
  • 1 tablespoon saffron threads
  • 1 teaspoon rose water (optional)
  • 1 cup water
    •

    Instructions

  • Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat.
  • Once the milk comes to a boil, reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add sugar, cardamom powder, chopped pistachios, chopped almonds, and saffron threads to the milk. Mix well and let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
  • In the meantime, prepare the paneer balls by kneading paneer until smooth and then shaping it into small balls.
  • In a separate pot, bring 1 cup of water to a boil. Add the paneer balls to the boiling water and let them cook for about 5-7 minutes. They will double in size.
  • Remove the cooked paneer balls from the water and gently squeeze out any excess water.
  • Add the cooked paneer balls to the flavored milk mixture and let them soak for at least 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  • Once chilled, garnish the Rasmalai with additional chopped pistachios and saffron threads.
  • Serve the Rasmalai chilled and enjoy!
