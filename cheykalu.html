CHEYKALU

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1/2 cup roasted gram flour (pottukadalai powder)
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
    •

    Instructions

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine rice flour, roasted gram flour, melted butter, sesame seeds, curry leaves, cumin seeds, salt, and red chili powder.
  • Add water gradually and knead into a smooth dough. The dough should be firm yet pliable.
  • Divide the dough into small lemon-sized balls and flatten them slightly using your palms.
  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai over medium heat.
  • Gently slide the flattened dough discs into the hot oil and fry them until they turn golden brown and crispy. Make sure to fry them in batches and do not overcrowd the pan.
  • Remove the fried Chekkalu using a slotted spoon and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  • Allow them to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
  • Serve Chekkalu as a snack with tea or enjoy them anytime!
