POHA

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups thick or medium-thick poha (flattened rice)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large potato, peeled and cubed
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Curry leaves
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ½ cup raw peanuts
  • Lemon juice (optional)
    •

    Instructions:

  • Rinse the poha in water and drain well.
  • Fry the peanuts in oil until crunchy and keep them aside.
  • In the same oil, splutter mustard seeds, add cumin seeds, and asafoetida.
  • Add chopped onion, green chilies, and cubed potatoes. Cook until the potatoes are tender.
  • Mix in turmeric powder, salt, and soaked poha. Stir well.
  • Add fried peanuts, lemon juice, and chopped coriander leaves. Mix gently.
  • Cover and let it sit for a couple of minutes to absorb the flavors.
