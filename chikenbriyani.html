CHICKEN BRIYANI

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (drumsticks and thighs)
  • Extra long Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, etc.)
  • Herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Ghee or oil
    •

    Instructions

  • Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  • Saut√© onions and tomatoes in ghee.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  • Parboil the rice.
  • Layer the chicken and rice in a pot.
  • Seal the pot with dough or foil.
  • Steam the biryani until fully cooked.
