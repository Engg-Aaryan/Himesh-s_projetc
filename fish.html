FISH CURRY

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish fillets (such as tilapia or cod)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
    •

    Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  • Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • Add fish fillets to the pan and gently mix with the masala.
  • Cover and cook for about 8-10 minutes or until the fish is cooked through.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with rice or bread.
