SEYMIAUPMA

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vermicelli (sevai)
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Curry leaves
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 medium carrot (finely chopped, optional)
  • 1 tablespoon grated coconut (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
    •

    Instructions:

  • 1.Roast the Vermicelli & Peanuts:
  • Dry roast 1 cup vermicelli (sevai) on low to medium flame until it turns light and slightly golden. Set it aside.
  • In the same pan, roast some peanuts until they become crunchy. Set them aside as well.
  • 2.Tempering & Sautéing
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan, Add 1 teaspoon mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, 1 tablespoon chana dal, and 1 tablespoon urad dal. Sauté until the dals turn golden.
  • Add a pinch of asafoetida (hing) and some curry leaves.
  • Add 1-2 chopped green chilies and 1 finely chopped onion. Sauté until the onion turns translucent.
  • If using, add finely chopped carrots and sauté for a few minutes.
  • 3.Cook the Sevai Upma:
  • Pour 2 cups of water into the pan, Add salt to taste and bring it to a boil.
  • When it starts boiling, add the roasted vermicelli, Mix well and cover it with a lid.
  • Cook until the water is absorbed (about 5-7 minutes), stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
